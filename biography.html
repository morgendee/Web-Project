My Biography

Early Life

I was born and raised in Ngong, Kenya, a beautiful town known for its scenic views and rich culture. Growing up in a supportive family with my parents Isaac and Alice Muriithi, I developed a strong foundation of values and work ethic from an early age.

Education Journey

My educational journey began at a local primary school where I discovered my passion for learning. I then attended Moi High School, where I excelled academically and participated in various extracurricular activities. Currently, at the age of 19, I am pursuing my higher education at Strathmore University in Nairobi, Kenya, where I am developing my skills and knowledge for a bright future.

Personal Interests

Beyond academics, I have diverse interests that shape my personality. I enjoy reading books to expand my knowledge, playing football for fitness and teamwork, listening to music for relaxation, coding to develop technical skills, traveling to explore new places, and photography to capture beautiful moments.

Aspirations

As a young student at Strathmore University, I am passionate about technology and innovation. I aspire to become a skilled software developer and entrepreneur, contributing to Kenya's growing tech ecosystem. My goal is to use my education and skills to make a positive impact in my community and beyond.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, I plan to complete my degree at Strathmore University, gain practical experience through internships, and eventually start my own technology company. I believe in the power of education and innovation to transform lives and communities.
